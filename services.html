Services

Virtual coaching. Real results. Built for fighters.

Fight Camp Programming

Full periodization design around your fight schedule. Pre-camp base building, in-camp intensity management, and peak week protocols. Designed to maximize performance on fight night without burning out mid-camp.

Off-Camp Strength Development

The off-season is where champions are built. Hypertrophy blocks, strength phases, and power conversion cycles structured to bring you into the next camp bigger, stronger, and more explosive.

Virtual 1-on-1 Coaching

Remote coaching via video check-ins, app-based programming, and direct message access. Full individualization, ongoing assessment, and program adjustments throughout your training cycle.

Injury Resilience & Return to Sport

Drawing on physical therapy training, Dylan designs programs that address common combat sport injuries — shoulder impingement, knee issues, hip mobility — and builds training capacity that keeps athletes on the mat.
