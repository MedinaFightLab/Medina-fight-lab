About Dylan Medina

The Mission

Better Combat Athletes

Dylan Medina is a Special Operations veteran, Strength & Conditioning coach, and Doctor of Physical Therapy student with a singular focus: building better combat athletes. His background in military service shaped a no-nonsense, evidence-driven approach to performance — one that transfers directly to the demands of fight sports.

Dylan operates at the intersection of exercise science and clinical knowledge, blending advanced S&C methodology with physiotherapy principles to develop fighters who are not just strong, but resilient, durable, and optimized for competition.

Through Medina Fight Lab, Dylan offers virtual coaching, fight-camp programming, and educational content for fighters and coaches who take their development seriously. Whether you're a competitive Muay Thai fighter, an MMA athlete, or a coach building a team system — Medina Fight Lab is built for you.
