Questions

Before You Book

Is this fully remote?

Yes. All coaching is delivered online through a training app, video check-ins, and messaging — so you can train at your own gym, anywhere.

What sports do you work with?

Muay Thai, boxing, MMA, kickboxing, BJJ, and grappling. If you compete or train seriously in a combat sport, the system applies.

Do I need a fully equipped gym?

No. Programs are built around the equipment you have access to. We sort that out on the intro call.

Are you a physical therapist?

Dylan is a Doctor of Physical Therapy student and an S&C coach. Coaching draws on that clinical knowledge for injury-resilient training, but is not a substitute for medical care or treatment of a diagnosed injury.

Can I cancel?

Yes — monthly coaching is month-to-month. Fight-camp blocks run for the length of the camp.
